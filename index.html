about

Darren is a coach and leader with extensive experience in building successful teams, transforming service delivery, trouble-shooting, public sector governance, digital transformation, and coaching individuals to unlock their potential. In our complex, fast-paced world, he is driven by a passion for creating simplicity, enabling high-functioning teams and generating powerful outcomes. His mission is to help others thrive in their life, work, and business.

Traditional coaching helps a person uncover what they need to do to move forward in a particular situation. Action steps are designed using the client’s current ways of seeing themselves and their world.

Rather than focusing on the what of the situation, Darren explores in partnership with the client how they’re relating to the situation. Simple but powerful shifts occur, enabling the client to make deep and lasting change.
